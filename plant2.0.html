Project Title:

Plant2.0

Project Partners:

Ben Geduld, Prachi Patil, Alex Saavedra

Project Sponsor:

Computer Engineering 346, Microprocessor System Design

Goal:

Our goal is to monitor and control factors affecting the growth of a basil plant with sensors and actuators entirely controlled with a Micro:bit. Using both digital and analog sensors, we will monitor atmospheric temperature and humidity, as well as soil moisture and absorbable light frequencies. We will use a water pump and colored LEDs to regulate those last two in response to sensor data.

Status:

In progress; anticipated completion 12/6/2022.
